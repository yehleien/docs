# Why Join Marginal Perspective?

## Target Users
Our platform is ideal for:
- Postsecondary students
- Automotive enthusiasts
- Professionals seeking to share expertise
- Anyone seeking verified perspectives

## Value Proposition

1. **Verified Perspectives**
   - Hear from others in your group with verified credentials
   - Trust that commenters are who they say they are

2. **Trusted Insights**
   - Get reliable information from people with verified experience
   - Connect with others who share your specific perspective

3. **Anonymous but Verified**
   - Share your views without revealing personal information
   - Maintain privacy while establishing credibility

4. **Universal Access**
   - Comment on any URL on the internet
   - Participate in discussions across all platforms
