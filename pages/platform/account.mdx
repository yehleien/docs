# Getting Started

## Account Creation
1. Visit our website to create an account
2. Provide necessary verification information
3. Choose and verify your initial perspectives

## Verification Process
1. Find your perspective in our system
2. Connect to relevant online portals
3. Submit required documentation
4. Complete verification checks

## Using Your Account

### Basic Usage
1. Paste any URL into our platform or use our browser addon
2. View comments from verified perspectives
3. Filter by perspective, view count, votes, etc.
4. Leave comments using your verified perspectives

### Commenting Rules
- Comment on entire URLs or specific sections
- Use one or multiple verified perspectives
- Engage with others sharing your perspectives
- Maintain anonymity while contributing
